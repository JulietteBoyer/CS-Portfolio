Who Am I?

My name is Juliette Boyer. I am fourteen years old and in ninth grade at Summit Shasta High School, home of the black bears. I am a level 10 competitive gymnast and I train at San Mateo Gymnastics in Belmont California. I train twenty four hours per week.

One of my favorite things is architecture. I have always loved designing and creating buildings and houses and would love to carry out my ideas on a bigger, more real scale. One of my goals is to graduate college with a degree in architecture or interior design.

Another one of my favorite things is reading. Growing up, reading was something that was given a high value. My favorite genre to read is science fiction because I find it fascinating and intriguing. One of my favorite books is The Hunger Games series.

One of my hobbies is gymnastics. I do gymnastics six days a week, four hours a day. It is not only really fun, but has taught me a lot that I can apply in my actual life as well as in the gym. My goal is to compete gymnastics for a division one college. I don't know what college I'd like to attend yet but some possibilities include, Auburn University, University of Denver, University of Washington, and Arizona State University.
